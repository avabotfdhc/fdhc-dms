## PART 2: WEBSITE PLATFORM RECOMMENDATION

### #1 Recommendation: Shopify ($29-79/month)

**Why Shopify is Best for This Business:**

✅ **Drop Shipping Integration**
- Oberlo app (for FashionTIY)
- DSers app (AliExpress products)
- Printful integration (custom designs)
- Auto-fulfillment with suppliers

✅ **Ease of Use**
- Drag-and-drop builder
- No coding required
- Mobile-responsive themes
- 24/7 support

✅ **Payment Processing**
- Built-in Shopify Payments
- PayPal integration
- Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Secure checkout

✅ **Marketing Tools**
- Built-in SEO features
- Email marketing integration
- Social media selling
- Abandoned cart recovery

✅ **Scalability**
- Handles growth automatically
- App ecosystem (6,000+ apps)
- Multi-currency support
- Inventory management

**Best Theme for Wild & Wonder:**
- **Dawn** (Free) - Clean, modern, fast
- **Refresh** (Free) - Great for lifestyle brands
- **Prestige** ($350) - Premium feel
- **Impulse** ($380) - Conversion optimized

---

### #2 Alternative: WooCommerce (WordPress)

**Cost:** Free plugin + Hosting ($10-30/month)

**Pros:**
- Lower ongoing costs
- More customization
- Own your data completely

**Cons:**
- Requires technical setup
- More maintenance
- Security responsibility

**Recommendation:** Start with Shopify, migrate to WooCommerce if you outgrow it and want lower costs.
