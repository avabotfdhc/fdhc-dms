## LOGO CONCEPT #10: Braxton's Adventure Colors

### Description:
The Signature B logo redesigned with Braxton's chosen colors - blue and red. The B features a bold blue with red accents (like a sunset behind mountains or a trail path). The colors work together to feel adventurous and kid-friendly.

### Visual Elements:

**The "B" Symbol:**
- **Main Color:** Deep adventure blue (navy or royal)
- **Accent:** Bright red for highlights/details
- **Mountain Peaks:** Blue with red "sunset" behind
- **Trail Path:** Red line winding through the B
- **Style:** Bold, confident, outdoor feel

**Color Placement:**
- Left side of B: Blue (cool, stable)
- Right side/accents: Red (energy, excitement)
- Or: Blue B with red shadow/outline
- Or: Gradient from blue to red (like sky to sunset)

### Why These Colors Work:

**Blue:**
- Trust, stability, outdoors
- Sky, water, adventure
- Appeals to boys and girls
- Professional but fun

**Red:**
- Energy, excitement, action
- Stand-out accent color
- Draws attention
- Kid-friendly and bold

**Together:**
- Classic adventure brand colors
- High contrast = visible from distance
- Gender-neutral appeal
- Timeless (not trendy)
