## BRAXTON'S STORY

**8 years old. Old soul. Big heart. Curious mind.**

Braxton doesn't just see problems - he fixes them. When something breaks, he's there with his tools. When something doesn't work right, he takes it apart to understand why. When kids don't have what they need, he wants to build a solution.

## PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT: The Braxton Method

**Step 1: Find the Problem**
"These shorts don't have pockets big enough for my rocks."

**Step 2: Design the Fix**
"What if we made deeper pockets with stronger stitching?"

**Step 3: Test It**
"I wore these for two weeks. Here's what worked and what didn't."

**Step 4: Improve**
"The pockets are good but the button came loose. Let's try a snap."

**Step 5: Only Then Sell**
"Now it's ready. I fixed all the problems."
