## PART 1: 90-DAY MARKETING LAUNCH PLAN

### PHASE 1: PRE-LAUNCH (Days 1-30)

#### Week 1: Foundation

- Finalize logo design
- Purchase domain (wildandwonder.com)

- Take sample photos

#### Week 2: Content Creation
**Day 8-10: Visual Content**

- Create 30 days of Instagram posts

- Identify 20 micro-influencers (mom bloggers, kid fashion)

- Create "Coming Soon" landing page

  1. "10 Adventure Activities for Kids This Summer"
  2. "How to Choose the Right Size for Your Child"
  3. "Why We Started Wild & Wonder"

#### Week 4: Final Preparations
**Day 22-25: Testing**
- Place test orders
- Check email notifications
- Test mobile responsiveness
- Review all product pages

**Day 26-28: Soft Launch**
- Launch to friends/family only
- Gather feedback
- Fix any issues
- Create urgency for public launch

**Day 29-30: Launch Prep**
- Finalize launch promotion
- Prepare email announcement
- Schedule social media countdown
- Set up Facebook/Instagram ads

## PART 4: OPERATIONS SETUP

### Order Fulfillment Process

**Step 1: Customer Places Order**
- Order appears in Shopify
- Customer receives confirmation email
- Payment processed

**Step 2: You Place Order with Supplier**
- Log into FashionTIY
- Find ordered products
- Add customer shipping address
- Pay wholesale price
- Mark as fulfilled in Shopify

**Step 3: Supplier Ships Direct to Customer**
- FashionTIY ships within 1-3 days
- You receive tracking number
- Add tracking to Shopify
- Customer receives shipping notification

**Step 4: Delivery & Follow-Up**
- Customer receives package
- Automated delivery confirmation
- 7-day follow-up email for review

**Timeline:**
- Order to ship: 1-3 days
- Shipping to customer: 7-15 days
- Total: 8-18 days
