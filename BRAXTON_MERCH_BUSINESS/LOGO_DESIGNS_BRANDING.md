## LOGO CONCEPT #1: Mountain Peak Adventure

### Description:
A stylized mountain peak with the letter "W" formed by two peaks meeting in the middle. A small sun or star sits above the peak. Below, "WILD & WONDER" in a friendly, rounded sans-serif font.

### Visual Elements:
- **Icon:** Two mountain peaks creating a "W" shape
- **Accent:** Small sun rising behind peaks
- **Typography:** Raleway Bold (for "WILD") + Raleway Regular (for "& WONDER")
- **Tagline:** "Where Little Adventures Begin" below in smaller text

### Color Variations:
1. **Primary:** Teal mountains (#1B6B7D) + Lime sun (#8AC540) on white
2. **Dark:** White mountains + lime sun on charcoal background
3. **Light:** Sage green mountains + mustard sun on cream

### Best For:
- Website header
- Clothing tags
- Business cards
- Social media profile pictures

### File Formats Needed:
- SVG (scalable)
- PNG with transparent background
- JPG for print
- Favicon version (just mountain icon)

## BRANDING GUIDE

### Primary Color Palette

**Brand Colors:**
- **Primary Teal:** #1B6B7D
  - Use for: Headers, buttons, links, primary logo
  - Psychology: Trust, calm, adventure

- **Accent Lime:** #8AC540
  - Use for: CTAs, highlights, accents, sale prices
  - Psychology: Energy, growth, fun

- **Neutral Cream:** #F8F7F4
  - Use for: Backgrounds, cards, sections
  - Psychology: Warmth, comfort, softness

- **Charcoal:** #2A2A2A
  - Use for: Text, headers, contrast
  - Psychology: Sophistication, readability

### Secondary Colors

- **Terracotta:** #C65D3B (fall accents, warmth)
- **Mustard:** #D4A017 (energy, attention)
- **Sage:** #87A878 (nature, calm)
- **Navy:** #1E3A5F (trust, depth)

### Typography

**Primary Font (Headings):**
- **Font:** Raleway Bold
- **Use:** Logo, headlines, navigation
- **Characteristics:** Modern, friendly, geometric

**Secondary Font (Body):**
- **Font:** Open Sans Regular
- **Use:** Body text, descriptions, emails
- **Characteristics:** Highly readable, neutral, professional

**Accent Font (Special):**
- **Font:** Pacifico or Satisfy (script)
- **Use:** Taglines, special announcements, quotes
- **Characteristics:** Playful, hand-written feel

### Brand Voice & Tone

**Voice Characteristics:**
- Warm and welcoming
- Adventurous but safe
- Encouraging and positive
- Knowledgeable but not preachy
- Fun but professional

**Tone Guidelines:**

**DO:**
- Use "we" and "our" to create community
- Encourage exploration and adventure
- Celebrate childhood wonder
- Be helpful and informative
- Use active, energetic language

**DON'T:**
- Talk down to parents
- Use fear-based marketing
- Be overly corporate or stiff
- Use slang that will date quickly
- Make promises we can't keep

**Example Copy:**

✅ **Good:** "Every adventure starts with the right gear. Our explorer tees are soft enough for naptime and tough enough for tree-climbing."

❌ **Avoid:** "Our products are made from high-quality materials and feature superior construction."

✅ **Good:** "Ready for backyard adventures? Grab a hat, pick a plush buddy, and let their imagination run wild!"

❌ **Avoid:** "Purchase our accessories to complete your child's outfit."

### Photography Style

**Image Guidelines:**
- Real kids (not models) in natural settings
- Outdoor environments: backyards, parks, trails
- Natural lighting preferred
- Candid moments over posed shots
- Diverse representation
- Show kids being kids (dirty knees, big smiles)

**Color Grading:**
- Warm, slightly desaturated
- Earth tones enhanced
- Natural skin tones
- Slight vintage/adventure feel

**Props:**
- Backpacks, binoculars, maps
- Stuffed animals as "adventure buddies"
- Nature elements: leaves, sticks, rocks
- Camping gear (kid-sized)

### Packaging Guidelines

**Shipping Packages:**
- Kraft paper mailers or boxes
- Teal or lime tissue paper inside
- Sticker seal with logo
- Thank you card with adventure tip

**Product Tags:**
- Kraft paper or recycled cardstock
- Logo on front
- Care instructions on back
- "Adventure Tip" (fun fact or activity idea)

**Insert Cards:**
- "Welcome to the Wild & Wonder family"
- Social media handles
- Discount code for next purchase
- Adventure activity suggestion

### Social Media Templates

**Instagram Post Template:**
- 1080x1080px
- Logo in corner or watermark
- Consistent filter/preset
- Hashtag block at bottom

**Instagram Story Template:**
- 1080x1920px
- Logo at top
- Product centered
- Swipe-up or link sticker

**Pinterest Template:**
- 1000x1500px (2:3 ratio)
- Title text overlay
- Logo watermark
- Product lifestyle shot

### Email Template Design

**Header:**
- Logo centered
- Navigation: Shop | About | Adventure Blog | Contact

**Body:**
- Single column layout
- Large product images
- Clear CTAs (teal buttons)
- Personal, conversational copy

**Footer:**
- Social media icons
- Physical address
- Unsubscribe link
- "Adventure Awaits!" sign-off
