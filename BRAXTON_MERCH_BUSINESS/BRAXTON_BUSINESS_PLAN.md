### Order Fulfillment Process

**With FashionTIY:**
1. Customer places order on your site
2. You receive payment (PayPal, Stripe)
3. You place order with FashionTIY (manual or automated)
4. FashionTIY ships directly to customer
5. You provide tracking number to customer
6. You keep the margin

**Timeline:**
- Order to ship: 1-3 days
- Shipping to customer: 5-15 days
- Total customer wait: 7-18 days

**With Honeydew USA:**
1. Customer places order
2. You receive payment
3. Email order to Honeydew (or use their portal)
4. They ship within 1-2 days
5. Customer receives in 3-7 days

---

### Customer Service Strategy

**Response Time Goals:**
- Email: Within 4 hours

**Policies to Establish:**
