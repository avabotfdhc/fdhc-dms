## LOGO CONCEPT #9: The Signature B

### Description:
A bold, stylized letter "B" that doubles as a mountain range. The letter is hand-drawn style with personality - not perfect, but confident. The curves of the B form two mountain peaks. "'s" is small, tucked beside the B like a signature. "WILD AND WONDER" sits below in clean, modern type.

### Visual Elements:

**The "B" Symbol:**
- Large, dominant letter B (60% of logo height)
- Top curve = first mountain peak
- Bottom curve = second mountain peak  
- Vertical line = mountain ridge
- Slight texture/distress for adventure feel
- Can stand alone as icon

**The "'s" Accent:**
- Small, positioned upper right of B
- Like a handwritten signature
- Creates ownership: "Braxton's"

**"WILD AND WONDER" Text:**
- Clean sans-serif below
- "WILD" slightly bolder than "AND WONDER"
- Balanced, centered under B

### Typography Specifications:

**The "B":**
- Font: Custom/hand-drawn or modified sans-serif
- Weight: Bold/Heavy
- Style: Slightly irregular, organic edges
- Feel: Confident, adventurous, personal

**The "'s":**
- Font: Script or handwritten
- Size: 20-30% of B height
- Position: Upper right, slightly overlapping
- Feel: Personal signature

**"WILD AND WONDER":**
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Montserrat, Raleway)
- Weight: Medium for "WILD", Regular for "AND WONDER"
- Tracking: Slightly expanded
- Case: ALL CAPS
